# Coupon Things
## `couponType`
Default: DVAT
- `T` - Treat Drop
- `S` - Micro Reward (e.g. SWAPS Reward)
- `F` - Free Drink
- `D` - DVAT (don't ask what that means, I have no idea)
- `B` - Birthday Cake
- `A` - Auto Offer
